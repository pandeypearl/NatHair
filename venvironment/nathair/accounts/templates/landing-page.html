How did we get here.

NatHair is a portfolio project for the foundation phase of the ALX/Holberton School Full Stack Programming course. This project was inspired by my own natural hair journey and the difficulties I personally faced when trying to find resources on how to better take care of my hair, what products to buy, and where I could find the products I needed for a reasonable price.

I wanted to create a place where you could access all the the information you need as well as be part of a community where people are going through the same dilemmas , therefore building a trusted community so you would not have to go through the natural hair journey alone. Thus NatHair was born.

- Pretty Pandey
